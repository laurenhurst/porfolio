A little about me

Hi, I'm Lauren Hurst. This website was created to share work completed during the MSGT program at the University of Washington along with previous passion projects.

My affinity for cartography and geospatial technologies grew from working within national parks and forests. I spent years in the field as a ranger, landscape photographer, environmental educator and content writer for outdoor brands before discovering cartography as a medium that combined both creative and computational interests. I hope that with the skills gained in graduate school, I will be able to combine photography, cartography, and coding to create applications and platforms that help increase awareness, care, and accessibility for Washington's public lands.
